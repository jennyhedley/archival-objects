Girl and Cat

Mother loved photography, me and cats

Here I am with my cat when we lived in the mountains. The 35mm film negative for this is possibly in storage. She might have developed the image herself. She, and later I, would convert our bathrooms to darkrooms.

One day this cat scaled a tall pine tree. It could have been one hundred years old. It was unclimbable, even for me. My mother called the fire department and they extended the ladder up to rescue our cat, who later had babies with her brother. She gave birth to a litter of six in my closet.

This photo would recreate itself many times

... and with many cats, most of whom were not mine.

These cats might have considered, however, that I was theirs.
